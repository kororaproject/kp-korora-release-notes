5. Changes in Fedora for Specific Audiences

5.1. Amateur Radio

5.1.1. CQRLOG

CQRLOG, an amateur radio general contact logging program, is making its introduction in Fedora 18. CQRLOG not only maintains a record of your contacts but also integrates with the ARRL Logbook of The World (LoTW), generates statistics on award completion, includes a DXCluster client, and many other features.
